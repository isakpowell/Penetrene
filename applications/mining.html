Common Applications

Electrical Protection

35,000V dielectric strength protects switchgear, junction boxes, and cable connections from moisture in underground environments.

Seized Fastener Removal

Creeps into corroded threads on conveyor frames, crusher liners, and structural steelwork to fracture rust bonds and free locked components.

Moisture Displacement

Displaces groundwater and condensation from exposed metal on contact, restoring electrical continuity and preventing accelerated corrosion.

Preventive Protection

Applied during shutdowns, leaves a lasting film that inhibits rust on structural steel, equipment frames, and stored components.
