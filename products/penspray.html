Built To Work
Where Others Can't

Deep-Creep Penetration

The low-viscosity formula wicks into hairline gaps, corroded threads, and seized joints, reaching where thick oils can't.

Corrosion Protection

Leaves a thin, tenacious protective film that repels moisture and inhibits rust, even after a single application.

Moisture Displacement

Instantly displaces water from electrical contacts, ignition systems, and metal surfaces to restore function fast.

Trigger Spray Control

Adjustable trigger delivers a precise, directed spray, covering large areas or focusing on a single fastener with equal ease.

Loosens Seized Fasteners

Penetrates rust bonds on bolts, nuts, and fittings, dramatically reducing the force needed to break them free.

Multi-Surface Safe

Safe for use on most metals, rubbers, and plastics. Won't swell seals or degrade rubber components under normal use.

Use

Common Applications

01

Seized Fasteners

Spray directly onto rusted bolts, nuts, and studs. The trigger allows targeted application without over-saturating.

02

Electrical Contacts

Short, controlled bursts displace moisture from connectors and ignition components without flooding sensitive electronics.

03

Hinges & Locks

Spray directly into hinge barrels and lock cylinders for precise lubrication with minimal mess.

04

Cables & Chains

Run the spray nozzle along chain links and cable runs for fast, even coverage across the full length.

05

Tools & Equipment

Quick spray-down of tool surfaces before storage and keeps hand tools and cutting equipment rust-free.

06

Marine Use

Spray onto fittings and deck hardware to protect against saltwater corrosion on vessels and trailers.

07

Workshop & Fabrication

Spray onto threads before cutting to prevent galling and ease disassembly of heat-treated components.

08

Automotive

Targeted spray on undercarriage fasteners, exhaust flanges, and brake hardware with no overspray on surrounding parts.
