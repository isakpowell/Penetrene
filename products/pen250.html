What It Does

Built To Work
Where Others Can't

Deep-Creep Penetration

The low-viscosity formula wicks into hairline gaps, corroded threads, and seized joints — reaching where thick oils can't.

Corrosion Protection

Leaves a thin, tenacious protective film that repels moisture and inhibits rust — even after a single application.

Moisture Displacement

Instantly displaces water from electrical contacts, ignition systems, and metal surfaces to restore function fast.

Long-Lasting Lubrication

Reduces friction on cables, hinges, chains, and moving parts — the film stays active long after application.

Loosens Seized Fasteners

Penetrates rust bonds on bolts, nuts, and fittings — dramatically reducing the force needed to break them free.

Multi-Surface Safe

Safe for use on most metals, rubbers, and plastics. Won't swell seals or degrade rubber components under normal use.

Use

Where It's Used

Common Applications

01

Seized Fasteners

Apply directly to rusted bolts, nuts, and studs. Allow to penetrate 2–5 minutes before attempting removal.

02

Electrical Contacts

Displaces moisture from connectors, switches, and ignition components to restore conductivity.

03

Hinges & Locks

Lubricates stiff door hinges, padlocks, and gate mechanisms. Apply sparingly and wipe excess.

04

Cables & Chains

Keeps brake cables, control lines, and drive chains lubricated and corrosion-free in outdoor conditions.

05

Tools & Equipment

Protects tool surfaces from rust during storage. Ideal for hand tools, power tools, and cutting equipment.

06

Marine Use

Protects fittings and exposed metal from saltwater corrosion on vessels, trailers, and dockside equipment.

07

Workshop & Fabrication

Prevents galling on thread-cutting operations and eases removal of heat-treated or press-fit components.

08

Automotive

Ideal for undercarriage components, suspension fasteners, exhaust flanges, and brake hardware.
